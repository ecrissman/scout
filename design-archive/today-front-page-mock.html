"Last light on the fence line." f/2.8 · 1/250 · ISO 400
The Editor's Note
A patient frame, held just long enough.

The rim light along the fence is the quiet engine of this picture — it does a lot of work without announcing itself. The negative space on the right almost gets away from you, but the horizon line saves it. One honest drag: the lede is buried; a tighter crop would push the eye to that edge-light sooner.
