Scout

A photo-a-day field journal. Editorial, not appy. Paper and ink. Quiet confidence.

Principles

  • Editorial, not appy. Scout behaves like a newspaper desk — dispatch datelines, stamps, field notes.
  • Paper and ink. Warm paper, deep ink, quiet type. Color shows up only at intentional moments.
  • Quiet confidence. No exclamation points. No emoji. Terse, literary, a little dry.
  • One thing at a time. One brief, one photo, one note per day.

Rules

  • Most of the app is ink on paper.
  • Press green only appears at earned moments (active timer, new assignment, note ready). Never on nav or chrome.
  • No gradients, no decorative shadows, no grain textures.
