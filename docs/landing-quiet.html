Brief 110 / 365

Scout is a small app for people who want to see more carefully. Every morning it hands you one assignment — a mood, an angle, a time box. You file one frame. No rolls, no drafts, no do-overs.

At eight in the evening, a short editor's note arrives. A read on what the frame did, and what to try tomorrow. The record accumulates, quietly, on the back page.
