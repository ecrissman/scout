The Practice

Three small moments, every day.

No. 01 · Morning

A brief.

Each day Scout sends one assignment — a mood, an angle, a time box. Small enough to carry in your pocket, specific enough to change how you see.

No. 02 · Anytime

A single frame.

One photo per day. No rolls, no drafts, no do-overs. File it by midnight. The limit is the lesson.

No. 03 · At 8pm

An editor's note.

A short read on what the frame did — and what to try tomorrow. Off the record, just for you. A daily conversation with the work.

Inside the App

Built like a photographer's notebook.

Editorial typography, paper-and-ink palette, a brief on the front page. Nothing that doesn't earn its place.

Don't wait for the app store

Add to home screen today.

Scout runs in the browser and installs to your phone's home screen like any native app. Same offline support, same daily brief. Open it on your phone:

scoutphoto.app
Step 01

Open in Safari

Go to scoutphoto.app on your iPhone. Chrome won't work — iOS only allows home-screen installs from Safari.

Step 02

Tap Share → Add to Home Screen

Hit the share icon at the bottom, scroll down, and tap "Add to Home Screen." Confirm with "Add."

Step 03

Launch from your icon

Scout opens full-screen like a native app. Your first brief arrives with it — ready in seconds.
