Available now on the web

You don't have to wait for the app store.

The iOS app ships this fall. Until then, Scout runs in Safari and installs to your home screen in two taps. Same daily brief, same offline archive, no account required to try it.

scoutphoto.app
  1. Open scoutphoto.app in Safari. iOS only allows home-screen installs from Safari, not Chrome.
  2. Tap the share icon, then "Add to Home Screen." Confirm with "Add."
  3. Tap the Scout icon on your home screen. It opens full-screen, like any app.
